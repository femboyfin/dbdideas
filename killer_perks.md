## [Blood Warden](<https://deadbydaylight.wiki.gg/wiki/Blood_Warden>) ğŸ—‘ï¸

- No longer blocks Exit Gates
  - This is the most controversial and relatively unhealthy portion of the perk. With no active way to identify it before it's activation, Blood Warden "scares" fundamentally change the survivors have to play end game even when the perk isn't in play. A Blood Warden activation in the last quarter of the end game collapse timer guarantees four kills and effectively holds all players in the trial for an additional minute when the outcome is already predetermined.
- Now reduces the End Game Collapse Timer by **30/40/50%** of it's remaining duration
  - Shifts Blood Warden to greatly increase the otherwise very limited pressure gained from an end game hook, while also encouraging multiple hooks during end game. This should effectively give you you a viable alternative to camping out each hook you get in end game, and make those last minute end game saves higher stakes. This also punishes survivors messing up an end game save rather than allowing 2-4 minutes of repeated save attempts (hook states allowing) to inch the hook location closer and closer to the gate.
- Added a **5 seconds** linger duration to the Exit Gate Aura reveal
  - Mildly increases the strength of the Aura reveal, this should prevent situations where the survivors ran out of the gate just in time before you could turn and look.


## [Bloodhound](<https://deadbydaylight.wiki.gg/wiki/Bloodhound>) ğŸ“‰ ğŸ—‘ï¸

- Increased duration to **3/4/5 seconds** *(was 2/3/4 seconds)*
  - Slight numbers buff, increasing this too much has the potential to make tracking *more* confusing as it can become harder to determine direction of travel from blood pools immediately.
- Now extends the Lifetime of Scratch Marks as well
  - Provides effective counterplay to Lightweight and Ghost Notes
- Now displays footstep imprints from walking and running Survivors
  - Leans into the tracking aspect of the perk, allowing you to follow the footsteps of walking survivors if you're close enough to see them, enabling you to find a stealthing survivor assuming you already found their approximate location confidently enough to start inspecting the ground.


## [Brutal Strength](<https://deadbydaylight.wiki.gg/wiki/Brutal_Strength>)

- Now grants **5% Haste** for **5 seconds** after completing a Break or Damage action
  - Slight buff without making the breaks themselves too overbearing, should help make up distance from breaking the pallet, eliminating slightly more room for error in survivor positioning around most pallets, making gen kick perks less time intensive to activate, and "mandatory-break" breakable walls (e.g. upstairs dead dawg saloon) less punishing to break at the start, potentially turning them into a utility of sorts.

## [Corrupt Intervention](<https://deadbydaylight.wiki.gg/wiki/Corrupt_Intervention>)

- Decreased duration to **70/80/90 seconds**
  - Slight nerf that now effectively guarantees other generators become available once a normal-speed generator is completed.
- Now reduces the duration by **60 seconds** per Health State lost
  - Punishes using a hit-and-run play style to gain more pressure than the existing deactivation condition was intended to prevent. Additionally, this should hit higher strength killers harder than lower strength killers by tying the deactivation to the time it takes you to injure survivors. The numbers on this may be slightly too aggressive, but given this is widely considered one of if not the best Killer perk in the game and the main benefit of Corrupt is forcing survivors to relocate and waste time running into blocked gens during the early game, not keeping them from doing specific gens for a set duration, Corrupt should still remain reasonably strong.

## [Dark Devotion](<https://deadbydaylight.wiki.gg/wiki/Dark_Devotion>) ğŸ“‰ ğŸ—‘ï¸

- Now activates when hitting any survivor with a Basic Attack
  - This is a reversion of the change made in [6.1.0](<https://deadbydaylight.wiki.gg/wiki/Patch_Notes_6.1.X#:~:text=Dark%20Devotion-,Now%20triggers%20when%20a%20Survivor%20loses%20a%20health%20state%20by%20any%20means%20(was%20just%20basic%20attacks>). Increases perk strength by separating it from specifically the Obsession, you should see more consistent activations of the perk throughout the trial. Additionally limiting it to Basic Attacks makes the perk slightly less oppressive on higher strength killers and encourages more deliberate and thoughtful activation them.
- Increased duration to **40/50/60 seconds** *(was 35/40/45 seconds)*
  - Sizable numbers buff, increasing the strength a bit by giving you more opportunities to catch someone off guard while your Terror Radius is elsewhere.
- Now activates only on Basic Attack
  - Prevents it from becoming too oppressive on killers with high strength and mobility chase powers (e.g. Blight who can both get a hit and reposition across the map rather quickly).
- While the perk is active, the last Survivor you injured with a Basic Attack receives your Terror Radius and it is set to **40 meters**, granting you the Undetectable status effect
  - Updated perk description to allow it's effects to be transferred to a different survivor for each subsequent basic attack within the same activation.
- Now has a cooldown of **60 seconds**
  - 1:1 activation to cooldown duration to keep it from becoming too overbearing on survivors.

## [Discordance](<https://deadbydaylight.wiki.gg/wiki/Discordance>)

- Now reveals the Aura of the Survivors working on the affected Generator, this lingers for **3/4/5 seconds** after they stop repairing

  - Makes it easier to tell at a glance how many people are stacking an affected generator.
- Removed Range limitation
  - Mild buff, effectively only removes edge-cases on very large maps. Made possible because we now have the Survivor Aura reveal as a variable stat.
